# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

Welcome to NexCV. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and AI-powered CV builder.

## 1. Information We Collect
*   **Account Information:** When you sign up, we collect your email address and name (e.g., via Google OAuth).
*   **CV Data:** The information you enter into the builder, including work experience, education, skills, and your profile image.
*   **Payment Information:** If you purchase a premium plan, your payment details are processed securely by our third-party payment gateway (PayHere). We do not store your credit card numbers on our servers.
*   **Usage Data:** We collect basic analytics (e.g., pages visited, templates used) to improve our service.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
*   To generate and store your CVs and PDFs.
*   To process payments and manage your premium subscription.
*   To send you transactional emails (e.g., password resets, payment receipts).
*   To improve our application and templates.

## 3. Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Third Parties
NexCV uses AI features (powered by Google Gemini) to parse your CV and generate professional summaries.
*   **Data Sharing:** When you use the AI features, relevant sections of your CV text are sent securely to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not use your personal data to train our own AI models.
*   **Cloud Storage:** We use AWS S3 to store custom templates and profile images.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
Your CV data is stored securely in our MongoDB database. You can delete your CVs at any time from your dashboard. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact support.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **support@nexcv.com**
