# Privacy Policy Draft

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

This is a working draft for NexCV and must be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before production use.

## 1. Information We Collect

NexCV may collect:

- Account information such as name, email address, password hash, Google OAuth identity details, role, plan, and verification status.
- CV content entered into the builder, including personal details, education, work history, skills, projects, languages, references, rich-text descriptions, and profile images.
- Saved CV document metadata, selected templates, design settings, and download activity.
- Billing and checkout records such as selected plan, coupon use, PayHere transaction references, Lemon Squeezy order/webhook references, payment status, currency, provider, and quota state. NexCV does not store card numbers.
- Support/contact information submitted through support tickets or contact forms.
- Technical and usage information such as session data, IP address, browser details, route activity, audit logs, and operational logs.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create, save, preview, and export CVs.
- Provide AI-assisted import, summary generation, and text refinement.
- Authenticate users and protect accounts.
- Process payments, activate plans, and enforce quotas.
- Send verification, password reset, support, billing, and administrative emails.
- Operate admin, support, billing, template, and audit workflows.
- Improve reliability, security, templates, and user experience.

## 3. AI Processing

NexCV uses Google Gemini for optional AI features. When a user chooses an AI feature, relevant CV text may be sent to Gemini for processing. NexCV does not train its own AI models on user CV data.

Users should avoid entering unnecessary sensitive information into AI prompts or CV text.

## 4. Third-Party Services

NexCV may use:

- MongoDB for application data storage.
- AWS S3 for template assets and related files.
- AWS Lambda or compatible infrastructure for PDF rendering.
- PayHere for local/LKR payment checkout and IPN notifications.
- Lemon Squeezy for global/USD payment checkout and order webhooks.
- SMTP, Gmail, or Resend for email delivery.
- Google OAuth for sign-in.
- Google Gemini for AI features.

Each third-party provider processes data under its own terms and policies.

## 5. Retention And Deletion

Users can delete saved CV documents and may delete their account through the application where available. Some records, such as payment, security, audit, or legal compliance records, may be retained for a limited period as required for operations, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or legal obligations.

Admin audit logs and operational logs may have separate retention rules.

## 6. Security

NexCV uses session cookies, access controls, rate limiters, admin permissions, optional admin IP allowlisting, and provider-side security controls. No internet service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.
